Lookinglook Records is an independent record label of electronic music, based in Kiev, Ukraine. It was started in January 2010.


The label main mission is to support talented producers and musicians of electronic music from all over the world, with further cooperation and realization of their creative potential.


Lookinglook Records is always looking for interesting electronic music, and if you want to send demo of your tracks, contact whith lable manager or buy Lookinglook's releases, please feel free to use this e-mail address:


lookinglookrecords@gmail.com


We speak English, Ukrainian and Russian.
